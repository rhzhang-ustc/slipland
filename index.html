How to Play

Controls

Press and hold to charge your jump, then release to launch. Longer hold = longer jump. You can only jump rightward.

Teetering? When you land near the edge (figure turns red), tap quickly to save yourself!

Physical Properties & Movement

Each platform has different friction. This affects how much you slide after landing:

  • Low friction (ice, metal): Slippery! You slide more—land with care or you may skid off the edge.
  • High friction (rubber, sandpaper): Grippy and forgiving. Less sliding, safer landings.
  • Medium friction (wood, plastic): Somewhere in between.

Land too fast and you'll skid. Land gently (low horizontal speed) for a safe stop and build a streak.
